Overview

Home Economics is a modern home-help and lifestyle solutions company that brings function, style, and ease to everyday living. Founded by an IIT Delhi alumnus, we believe household management shouldn’t feel like a second job — it should be seamless, smart, and even delightful. We integrate AI-driven tools to personalize and streamline home solutions.

What We Do

We help urban households solve practical, day-to-day domestic issues—be it a cluttered kitchen, poor lighting, messy wardrobes, or uninspired home aesthetics. We deliver curated solutions combining design, efficiency, affordability, and tech-enabled insight.

  • Space Optimization & Decluttering
  • Aesthetic Decor & DIY Interiors
  • Smart Home Product Recommendations
  • Utility Planning (Lighting, Storage, Furniture)
  • Weekly/Monthly Home Wellness Audits
  • Personalized Setup for Renters & Bachelors
  • AI-Based Layout & Product Recommendations
  • Routine & Utility Optimization via Lifestyle Mapping

Why Home Economics?

  • Because good design should work just as hard as you do.
  • Because your home deserves a plan, not patchwork.
  • Because adulting is hard enough—let us make your space easier to live in.
  • Because AI can now help you plan smarter than ever before.

Our Approach

We don’t just send carpenters or stylists. We send Home Strategists—trained, creative problem-solvers who understand lifestyle, aesthetics, and budgets. Backed by intelligent systems and recommendation engines, we offer solutions that are both personal and data-driven.

Our Clients

From working professionals and young couples to students, expats, and elderly parents living alone—we serve all those who value their time, space, and peace of mind. Whether you’re setting up your first home or looking to make your old space feel new again, we bring clarity to your chaos.

Our Promise

No jargon. No chaos. No costly overhauls. Just honest, effective solutions tailored to your home and habits—with a dash of smart tech where it counts. A better home, minus the headache.
