Home Care Range

Powerful. Safe. Naturally Effective.

Our Home Care Range brings together science, scent, and sensibility—crafted to make every corner of your home shine with effortless freshness. Each product is powerful yet gentle, designed to clean deeply, smell divine, and keep your space truly cared for.

Formulated with safe, high-quality ingredients and infused with subtle natural fragrances, these cleaners go beyond hygiene—they elevate your routine. It's cleanliness that's not just seen, but felt.

1. FABRIC CLEANER - Gentle on Clothes, Tough on Dirt

Our Fabric Cleaner effectively removes stains and odour while preserving the natural texture and colour of your clothes. Enriched with plant-based cleansing agents and light essential oils, it ensures freshness without harsh chemicals.

Usage:
  • Mix in a 1:1 ratio with water before use.
  • Suitable for both machine wash and hand wash.
  • For stubborn stains, use a slightly higher concentration directly on the spot before washing.

Gentle | Non-toxic | Biodegradable

Available in 250mL and 500mL bottles.

2. FLOOR CLEANER (CONC.) - Deep Clean, Naturally Fresh

Our Floor Cleaner is a concentrated blend of natural surfactants and disinfecting essential oils that eliminate germs, dirt, and odour—without the need for harsh phenyl or bleach.

Usage:
  • Dilute 2-3 caps of cleaner in 1L of water.
  • Use the diluted solution as required in your cleaning bucket with water.
  • Safe for marble, tiles, and all common floor types.

Child-safe | Pet-friendly | Chemical-free

Available in 250mL and 500mL bottles.

3. TILE CLEANER - Brightens, Revives, Restores

This powerful Tile Cleaner is designed to tackle hard water marks, soap scum, and tough floor stains without using harsh acids. It restores brightness and sheen, keeping your tiles fresh and smooth.

Usage:
  • Apply a small amount directly on the stain.
  • Rub gently with a cloth or brush until the stain loosens.
  • Rinse thoroughly with plenty of water - do not leave the cleaner on the surface without washing, as prolonged contact may cause damage.

Harmful Acid-free | Fast-acting | Restorative care

Available in 250mL and 500mL bottles.

4. TOILET CLEANER - Hygiene That Respects Your Home

Our Toilet Cleaner uses a natural, balanced formula with antibacterial essential oils to remove stains, kill odour-causing bacteria, and leave your bathroom smelling refreshingly clean.

Usage:
  • Apply directly under the rim and across the bowl.
  • Leave for 5-10 minutes, scrub, and flush clean.

Non-acidic | Safe for septic tanks | Eco-conscious

Available in 250mL and 500mL bottles.

5. GLASS AND WINDOW CLEANER - Clarity, Naturally

Formulated with vinegar extract and citrus-based solvents, our Glass Cleaner clears dust, grease, and smudges without leaving streaks or ammonia fumes.

Usage:
  • Spray lightly and wipe with a clean microfiber or cotton cloth for streak-free brilliance.

Streak-free | Ammonia-free | Quick-dry

Available in 250mL and 500mL bottles.
