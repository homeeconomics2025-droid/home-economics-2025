Overview

Home Economics is a modern home-help and lifestyle solutions company that brings function, style, and ease to everyday living. Founded by an IIT Delhi alumnus, the company is built on the belief that household management shouldn’t feel like a second job—it should be seamless, smart, and even delightful. We enhance this experience further by integrating AI-based tools to personalize, simplify, and streamline home solutions.

What We Do

We help urban households solve practical, day-to-day domestic issues—be it a cluttered kitchen, poor lighting, messy wardrobes, or uninspired home aesthetics. But we do it differently: through carefully curated solutions that blend design, efficiency, affordability, and tech-enabled insights.

  • Space Optimization & Decluttering
  • Aesthetic Decor & DIY Interiors
  • Smart Home Product Recommendations
  • Utility Planning (Lighting, Storage, Furniture)
  • Weekly/Monthly Home Wellness Audits
  • Personalized Setup for Renters & Bachelors
  • AI-Based Layout & Product Recommendations
  • Routine and Utility Optimization via Lifestyle Mapping
